Duran Goodyear - Founder / Principal

Duran Goodyear is a recognized web application architect having been involved in the development of websites from Fortune 100 companies to major universities across the country. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, Duran Goodyear founded Alphex Information Solutions with the goal of providing exceptional technology solutions to mission driven organizations across the planet. In partnership with award winning individuals and organizations, Duran continues that mission to this day and is excited by technology and the ways it can be used to answer his clients needs every day.

Matt Callahan - Technology Director

Matthew Callahan is a full-stack web developer with professional experience that includes mobile applications, custom business software, SCORMbased eLearning development, and CMS-driven web applications. With a background in formal programming and a degree in Digital Media from Drexel University, Matthew is skilled in and passionate about both backend and front-end development. In addition to coding, his interests also include usability and user experience. Matthew has been working with Drupal for nearly a decade and has launched sites for a variety of clients ranging from small non-profits to large universities and national museums.

Tim Beitz - Creative Director

Tim Beitz leads creative direction and design for Alphex. For over a decade he has brought value to brands through print, digital, and experience design, working with clients such as Nike, UGG Australia, The Philadelphia Eagles, The American Cancer Society, The University of Florida, University of the Arts, and many other higher education institutions. He has held several positions at the Philadelphia-based branding and creative services firm 160over90, also developing the User Experience approach within the Interactive division. His work has been recognized by the AIGA and Print Magazine. Recent sites have been designed for Dickinson College, Fairfield University, University of the Arts, and the University of Florida.
